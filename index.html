Fiddlers

The colony of Ye Old Fiddlers was established by our forefathers El Bigsgato III and Ex-Bandito Matzollo somewhere in Mexico many centuries ago. Some time between then and now the survivors of the colony migrated to England and carried on their blood lines. The descendants of the forefathers where mystically brought back together in approximately the year 2006 where they started the modern day equivelant of Ye Old Fiddlers known as The Fiddlers. Over the years The Fiddlers acquired new members and expanded to the larger family known under the banner of FiddleMy.Wang and this is where you are today.
